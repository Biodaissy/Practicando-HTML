Salad
  • 2 cups of chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup of fresh spinach
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 avocado, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of sunflower seeds
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce and fresh spinach.
  2. Add the cucumber, grated carrot, tomato, and avocado to the lettuce and spinach mixture.
  3. Sprinkle the sunflower seeds and crumbled feta cheese over the salad.
  4. In a separate container, mix the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and gently toss to ensure all the ingredients are well coated.
  6. Serve the healthy salad and enjoy it immediately.
Breakfast
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • a handful of fresh spinach
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. In a non-stick skillet, cook the eggs to your preference (scrambled, fried, or as an omelet). Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Meanwhile, cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh. Mash it with a fork until smooth.
  3. Toast the slices of whole wheat bread.
  4. To assemble the healthy breakfast, place one toasted bread slice on a plate. Spread a layer of mashed avocado onto the bread.
  5. Add the fresh spinach on top of the avocado and place the tomato slices over it.
  6. Place the cooked eggs on the tomato and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Cover with the second toasted bread slice.
  8. Serve the healthy breakfast accompanied by a fresh fruit of your choice and enjoy.
Dessert
  • 1 cup of unsweetened Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened granola
  • 1 cup of fresh fruits (strawberries, blueberries, mango, etc.), cut into pieces
  • Optional: fresh mint leaves for garnish
  1. In a small bowl, mix the Greek yogurt and honey or maple syrup. Stir well to combine and lightly sweeten the yogurt.
  2. In individual glasses, start assembling the parfait. Place a layer of sweetened yogurt at the bottom of each glass.
  3. Add a layer of granola on top of the yogurt.
  4. Layer the cut fresh fruits over the granola.
  5. Repeat steps 2 to 4 to create additional layers of yogurt, granola, and fruits until the glasses are filled or until the ingredients are used up.
  6. Decorate the top of the parfait with some additional fresh fruits and mint leaves, if desired.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to blend and the granola to slightly soften.
  8. Serve the yogurt and fruit parfait as a healthy dessert and enjoy its delicious combination of textures and flavors.
